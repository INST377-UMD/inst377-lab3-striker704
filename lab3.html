How to Play Rock-Paper-Scissors

Rules:

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock
  • Same choice = tie

Instructions:

  1. Enter your name
  2. Select Rock, Paper, or Scissors from dropdown
  3. Click "Play Game" to begin
  4. The computer plays too
  5. Winner wins the round
